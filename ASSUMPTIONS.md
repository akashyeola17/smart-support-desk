1) Authentication is session-based and simplified for demonstration purposes.
2) Passwords are stored in plain text for simplicity
3) Priority is derived from a predefined priority matric.
4) SLA timings are calculated based on priority at ticket creation.
5) Ticket status follows a fixed lifecycle and invalid transitions are not allowed.
6) Comments are treated as a conversation between user and admin.
7) Internal vs Public comments are not implemented.
8) Admin role is predefined in the database
9) SLA countdown is calculated dynamically on page load.
